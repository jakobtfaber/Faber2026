2 · The headline: the wrong-PBF α artifact depends on bandwidth lever arm

Left: fitting our current EMG to a heavy-tailed injection with true α=4.67 — the joint CHIME+DSA fit and CHIME-only fit recover α to −0.06; the DSA-only fit (15% fractional bandwidth) collapses below 4 with wide errors, exactly the Cordes Fig-58 artifact. Sub-4 scattering indices from narrow-band single-instrument fits are suspect as a class; our joint methodology is immune. Right: the joint-fit α bias from EMG mismatch is bounded (≈−0.03 as S/N→∞) and shrinks with si because the far tail sits below the noise floor — the EMG-vs-PL discrimination lives in the near tail (t/τe ~ 3–15). Consequence: the PL-PBF change moves residual structure and evidence, not the headline α or TOAs — the β>3.98 limit is robust to PBF-shape systematics.
