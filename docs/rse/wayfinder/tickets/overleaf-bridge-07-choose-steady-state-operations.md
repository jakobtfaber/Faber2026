## Question

After a successful manual activation, what routine operating contract prevents
recurrence and silent divergence? Decide who or what initiates each direction,
how freshness and single-writer ownership are proved, which size/dependency/
compile/PDF checks run every time, where receipts and failures are surfaced,
how credentials rotate, and when the bridge must fail closed and require a new
reviewed reconciliation.
