# Standing delegated decision authority for recorded open tickets

**Granted:** 2026-07-20 local / 2026-07-21 UTC

**Scope anchor:** `main` commit `33e9e1ce357073c78f6a4aaf7138b3e03e9c87da`

**Status:** active

## Owner grant

> I delegate decision authority to Codex for all Wayfinder tickets recorded as
> open at main commit 33e9e1ce3570. Codex may adopt and implement its
> evidence-backed recommended option without separate per-ticket approval, then
> resolve the ticket only after its acceptance and validation checks pass.
>
> This delegation does not waive fail-closed evidence requirements, independent
> validation, or required owner visual review. It does not preauthorize data
> deletion or movement, irreversible Git operations, service changes,
> manuscript submission, Figure 3 promotion, scientific trust promotion,
> foreground redshift or budget re-adjudication, or coauthor changes. Those
> require an exact proposal or completed review artifact before approval.
>
> Codex must stop when evidence is missing, independent checks disagree, the
> recommendation would materially change a scientific claim, or the action
> exceeds the ticket's recorded scope. New tickets are not covered
> automatically.

## Operational interpretation

- This is delegated authority to choose an evidence-backed recommendation, not
  advance approval of an unknown result.
- It does not claim, close, unblock, or otherwise change any ticket by itself.
- Ticket blocking, acceptance criteria, validation, and strict completion
  semantics remain unchanged.
- A covered ticket's resolution must cite this grant and the evidence that
  supports the selected recommendation.
- Any listed exception remains a fresh owner gate even when the underlying
  ticket is covered.
- Tickets created after the scope anchor require a new grant or explicit
  per-ticket approval.
