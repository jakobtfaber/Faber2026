# Research: Outdated science-result quarantine

**Date:** 2026-07-17
**Scope:** Internal codebase
**Related Documents:** [Provisional propagation handoff](../handoff/handoff-2026-07-15-16-49-provisional-propagation-next.md)
**State inspected:** Faber2026 `33ecbb66`; pinned pipeline `17d9d266`; pipeline `origin/main` `8f5f06a`

## Question / Scope

Determine how to remove obsolete science numbers from current-looking and
compiled surfaces without deleting their bytes or losing the commands and
inputs needed to re-examine them later. The scope is limited to the stale
surfaces identified in the July 17 science audit. Already explicit archival
figures and the reproducible legacy-energy cross-check remain in place.

## Codebase Findings

### Compiled manuscript surfaces

- `sections/results.tex:177-191` still introduces a provisional DSA catalog and
  pre-production scattering values as inputs to future screen attribution.
- `sections/results.tex:239-245` and `sections/results.tex:333-344` compile the
  provisional DSA and pre-PL-PBF joint-fit tables.
- `sections/discussion.tex:82-115` converts provisional free-alpha scattering
  values into event-specific foreground fractions and causal readings. This is
  the highest-risk stale surface because it is interpretive, not merely
  historical.
- `scripts/build_provisional_propagation_tables.py:260-284` writes all three
  obsolete tables back to the manuscript root, so moving the committed files
  without redirecting the generator would be temporary.

### Current-looking pipeline surfaces

- `analysis/beta_campaign/two_screen.py:1-23` and `:180-221` generate the

- `results/joint_fit_summary.md` is generated by
  `analysis/scattering-refit-2026-06/gen_joint_summary.py:19-28`, whose embedded

- `galaxies/foreground/attribution_matrix.py:30-35` hard-codes legacy
  bandwidths, while `:219-229` writes the resulting matrix to the canonical
  foreground-data directory.
- `analysis/chime-scintillation/README.md:1-41` and `INVENTORY.yaml:1-4` claim
  that no qualified CHIME measurement exists, predating the finalized campaign.

### Repository boundary

Faber2026 `origin/main` pins pipeline `17d9d266`. Pipeline `origin/main` is two
commits ahead at `8f5f06a`; the intervening changes are the fail-closed model-ToA
correction and the finalized results-library index. A pipeline quarantine must
therefore land as its own commit/PR, followed by a deliberate parent gitlink
update whose full `17d9d266..new-pin` delta is validated.

### Historical matrix reproducibility gap

A fresh build of the old attribution algorithm no longer matches the preserved
CSV: registry/input drift changes at least the Wilhelm coherence and DM-budget
cells. The old byte-parity test therefore cannot honestly survive quarantine.
The replacement guard checks that the twelve-row snapshot is preserved and
inspectable, while unit tests may continue exercising the historical algorithm
without claiming that it regenerates a current artifact.

## Synthesis

A reversible quarantine needs three properties: preserve exact stale bytes,
remove their live/compiled reachability, and redirect producers so reruns do not
recreate obsolete files at their former paths. A dated quarantine with a
machine-readable path map satisfies those requirements while leaving later
deletion or rehabilitation as an explicit owner decision.

The active `twoscreen_provisional_table.tex` is not obsolete: it encodes the
accepted fail-closed policy and contains no numerical screen verdicts. It must
remain compiled. The legacy energy and already suppressed figure families are
also excluded because their current provenance labels already prevent use as
live results.
